Questions You Might Have

Straight answers, no surprises

Do I have to write the messages myself?

Nope. We draft every message using your client's home purchase history—how long they've owned, their neighborhood, even local news. You just review and hit send.

Will it sound like a robot?

Not at all. Every message is editable, and it sends from your actual phone number. Your clients think it's you being thoughtful (which it is—we just help with the remembering part).

What real estate CRMs does HiAgain work with?

We currently integrate with Follow Up Boss. KVCore/Lofty and other popular real estate CRMs are on our roadmap. Don't see your real estate CRM? We also support CSV import.

Does this work for buyer clients or just sellers?

Both. Whether you helped them buy their first home or sell their condo, HiAgain keeps the relationship warm.

Can I change how often it sends?

Absolutely. Default is every 90 days (that's the sweet spot for staying in touch without being annoying), but you can customize the rhythm for different types of past clients.

What if I want to cancel?

Cancel anytime, no contracts, no hard feelings. We figure if you're not getting referrals and repeat business from this, you shouldn't pay for it.
